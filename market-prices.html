Price Trends and Analysis

As shown in Figure 3.2, maize prices have shown typical seasonal variation, with lowest prices during harvest (August-September) and gradual increases during the lean season. Farmers are advised to consider storage and marketing strategies to maximize returns. The current prices represent a 15% increase from harvest-time lows.

Market Intelligence Tips

  • Prices are typically 15-20% higher in urban markets (Accra, Kumasi) compared to production areas
  • Wednesday and Saturday markets generally have better price discovery
  • Consider collective marketing through farmer groups for better bargaining power
  • Quality sorting and grading can increase prices by 10-30%
  • Transportation costs average GHS 15-20 per bag from farm to regional market

Data Source: Ministry of Food and Agriculture (MoFA) Market Information System, updated weekly.
